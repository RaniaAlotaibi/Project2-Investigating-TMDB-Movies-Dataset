The second research question'what are the highest 5 movies in profit?': According to what the shows, top one movie that title Avatar had a largest profit reaching 2.5 billion.

The forth research question 'what are the top 5 director earned highest profit?': According to what the shows, the first director 'James Cameron' has gain the significant profits,James Cameron the director of Avatar movie as we see befor.
